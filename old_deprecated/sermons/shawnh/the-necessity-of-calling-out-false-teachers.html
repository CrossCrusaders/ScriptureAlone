About Shawn H.

Shawn H: A Teen for the Kingdom of God

Shawn grew up in a Christian household and was raised in the Brethren denomination. He became a false convert at the age of 7 due to fear of dying and going to hell and continued to live in sin and wickedness throughout his early teen years despite the fact that he was homeschooled. On April 2019, Shawn became suicidal and was about to end it all when the Lord alerted his mother who tended to him and helped him on the road to recovery. Shawn may have had no friends in real life or online anymore, but the Lord had great plans in store for him.

He was called to ministry 2 months later and started Cross Crusaders (at the time branded FoxyGlobalGamingMinistries) on June 12th, 2019. FGGM was created because Shawn had felt the burden to share the gospel with the youth through online video game sessions, however, he did not consult the Lord in the early stages regarding his burden and started things on his own. Due to his lack of inexperience and no one to mentor him through this process, Shawn did the best he could with the little he knew. This led to several unbiblical action such as working with false denominations and preaching a false Gospel in order to increase the ministry size. Shawn was rebuked late 2019 by two elderly men named Bruce and Vladmir upon which Shawn repented and started to learn more about sound doctrine and seperating from the world. From late 2020 to late 2021, he lost 24 partnerships, 26 staff members, and became an outcast from many of his friend circles due to his "controversial" stance on following sound doctrine.

Shawn did rename the ministry in January 2021 to FoxyGlobalEvangelicalMissions, however, in mid-2021 he realized that he did not belong in the evangelical community and hence changed the name to Cross Crusaders. With careful consideration and prayer, Shawn decided to become a part of the Independent Fundamentalist Baptist community and has determined to follow the will of God for his life and to bring the fundamentalist community back to what it used to be and get true biblical preaching, sound doctrine, sound pastors, and doctrinally sound churches in the eyes of the public society. His goal is to unite the fundamentalists through the truth of the Word of God and to bring an end to this modernist chaos unraveling in the churches around us today.
