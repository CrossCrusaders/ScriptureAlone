About Spencer Smith

Spencer Smith: Husband, Father of Four, Christian YouTuber, and most importantly...a fundamentalist missionary.

Spencer Smith's life didn't start out so great. He grew up in an unsaved family and lived in drunkenness and sin throughout his teen years. At the age of 18, his youth pastor preached a sermon which changed the heart and life of Spencer forever. He received the calling to go to bible college and become a missionary to the people of Kenya. There he, along with other fundamentalist missionaries, founded Lighthouse Baptist Missions to bring the true Gospel to the lost people of Kenya. He started his YouTube channel in September 2007, but Spencer and his channel started to become known through his well-made documentary series Third Adam (1, 2, 3, & 3X). He continues to call out false teachers and bring out the truth in the modern, false Christianity that is spreading more rampant than ever. He occasionally posts sermons from his church as well as a mini-series called "Morning Devotions" that he does for slight humor.
